# Boreas V3 Product Context

## What This Is

Boreas sells a done-for-you digital consultorio for private medical practices. The offer is not "a web page"; it is a 24/7 trust filter that helps a doctor turn online reputation into qualified WhatsApp conversations.

## Audience

- Private doctors and clinic owners in Mexico.
- Specialists with Google Maps reviews, WhatsApp demand, and limited time.
- Practice managers who care about fewer low-intent messages and faster patient trust.

## Hormozi Offer Logic

- Dream outcome: a professional consultorio digital open 24/7 that reflects the doctor's essence and filters decided patients.
- Certainty: use existing Google Maps reputation, real patient reviews, and professional medical copywriting.
- Time delay: delivery in 48 to 72 hours.
- Effort: one WhatsApp audio of one minute. No forms, no copywriting, no technical work from the doctor.

## Conversion Job

Make the visitor feel: "This is a smart, low-risk business decision, and I can start without extra work." The primary conversion is contact by form/WhatsApp for follow-up.

## Public Offer Rules

- Do not show the flash price.
- Do not show weekly scarcity or "last slot" publicly.
- Keep urgency tied to patient behavior: nights, weekends, and patients searching before booking.

## Relevo Role

Relevo is secondary. It appears near the end as a quiet upsell for doctors who already have a website and want WhatsApp automation.

## Voice

- Spanish first.
- Clear before clever.
- Calm, professional, and direct.
- Use words doctors and assistants recognize: agenda, pacientes, WhatsApp, Google Maps, reseñas, consultorio.

## Non-Goals

- No generic SaaS feature selling.
- No purple/default AI visual language.
- No crowded multi-CTA hero.
- No public pricing or public scarcity claims.
- No internal build jargon.
